About Our Masjid

Al-Hilal Islamic Center was legally established in March 2020. The Center is a Non-Profit, Educational, Religious and Humanitarian Organization.

Masjid Alhilal is committed to the Islamic way of life based on the Qur’an and life example of Prophet Muhammad. We serve as a center for religious development, social, educational, economic, and cultural enrichment. We believe in the Oneness of God (Allah) and the oneness of humanity. We are committed to promoting excellence in community life and human dignity throughout our neighborhoods, city, country, and global community.
